Speakers

Jason Swenk

JFounder, Jason Swenk Consultancy

After a short dip in the corporate world, Jason Swenk knew he wanted to run the show. He opened his first digital marketing agency and served heavy hitters AT&T, Hitachi, and Lotus Cars. He scaled the agency to eight figures fast and sold it within 12 years. He now teaches growth-minded agencies his 8-system framework for scaling to millions. Jason brings more than two decades of industry experience to his podcast, the Smart Agency Master Class Podcast, co-owns top agency Republix, and crashes remote-controlled planes on the weekends.

Jody Sutter

Founder, The Sutter Company

Jody Sutter started her career “dialing for dollars” in a sales position. The resiliency and sales skills she gained became a huge asset in the agency space. She spent nearly two decades leading business development at agencies like Havas and R/GA before starting her firm, The Sutter Company.

She now supports small marketing agencies through coaching, workshops and hands-on programs. As an agency veteran, Jody quickly identifies the challenges young agencies face. Her outsider perspective gives agencies the insight to understand their strengths and how to position their brand to win more deals.

JC Hite

Founder & CEO, Hite Digital International

JC Hite had a cozy position with an Inc. 500 agency. But he knew his purpose was bigger than the dollar-driven metrics most agencies strive toward. Soon after handing in his resignation letter, he founded Hite Digital from his spare bedroom.

Hite Digital International is now one of the fastest-growing agencies in the U.S. JC quickly scaled to 19 offices in North and Central America and created a model that enables faster growth through franchising. He contributes to several online publications and leads the Digital Agency Hacker, which encompasses his Inner Circle, podcast and book.

Lee McKnight Jr.

Vice President of Sales, RSW/US

After a short dip in the corporate world, Jason Swenk knew he wanted to run the show. He opened his first digital marketing agency and served heavy hitters AT&T, Hitachi, and Lotus Cars. He scaled the agency to eight figures fast and sold it within 12 years. He now teaches growth-minded agencies his 8-system framework for scaling to millions. Jason brings more than two decades of industry experience to his podcast, the Smart Agency Master Class Podcast, co-owns top agency Republix, and crashes remote-controlled planes on the weekends.

Lee specializes in new business development, lead gen, consulting, content, social media, and account management. If you’re lucky, you may also catch his band in a show around Louisville and Cincinnati.

Landon Ray

Founder & CEO, Ontraport

Ontraport Founder and CEO Landon Ray is a serial entrepreneur whose personal mission is to educate, motivate and enable others to realize their goal of starting and growing their own business.

At age 25, Landon transformed himself from a street-corner flower vendor into one of the nation’s top securities day traders in only 12 months. After beating the odds on Wall Street, Landon took his personal experience as an entrepreneur and created Ontraport, a CRM and automation platform with a mission to make it easier for entrepreneurs to bring their value to the world.

With a constant beat on what small businesses need to compete and grow, Landon has continually innovated new features that have made Ontraport the most robust, customizable marketing and sales platform in the small business market.

Brendan Dubbels

Senior Partner Manager, Ontraport

Brendan Dubbels takes pride in being the fifth hire at Ontraport and is a self-proclaimed startup fanatic. He’s managed several aspects in the company over the past 13 years and currently oversees partnerships and business development. His main focus is on implementing data-driven strategies to fuel sales and create customers for life.

Brendan established the company’s Email Delivery team, which has overseen the sending of over 12 billion emails on behalf of our customers, and implemented sales strategies that significantly decreased first-response time and drove outbound sales. In his downtime, Brendan is a coffee-roasting aficionado, and founded Good Girl Coffee, named after his dog Kona.
